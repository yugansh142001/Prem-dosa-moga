About Us

Welcome to Prem's Dosa Hub

We are a 100% vegetarian restaurant dedicated to serving authentic South Indian cuisine with a modern twist. Our passion is to bring the rich flavors of South India to your table with the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes.

Located in the heart of Moga, Punjab, we pride ourselves on quality, freshness, and exceptional customer service. Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail.
